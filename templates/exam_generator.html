Introduction Summary

A university in Resistencia-Chaco requires a system to create examination schedules for its students. The university has provided the following requirements that must be fulfilled:

Requirements:

Examination Periods

  • There are two examination periods:

    • First Call and Second Call.

  • Examinations are not scheduled on holidays and weekends.

Data Entry

The system should include a data entry feature to input the following information:

  • Start and end dates for the First Call.

  • Start and end dates for the Second Call.

Examination Time Slots

  • Examinations are divided into two shifts:

    • Morning Shift: 8:00 AM to 11:00 AM.

    • Afternoon Shift: 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM.

Examination Scheduling Constraints

  • A day cannot have two exams of the same year.

  • The academic program consists of 5 years.

  • Separate documents should be generated for each year.

  • First and second-year exams only take place in the morning.

  • Exams for the remaining years take place in the afternoon, except for two courses in the third year:

    • Tecnicas Kinesicas 4

  • Between a subject that has correlatives, it has to be at least 5 days. Example: Tecnica 1 10/12, Tecnica 2 15/12.

File Format

  • The generated files should be in Excel or CSV format.
